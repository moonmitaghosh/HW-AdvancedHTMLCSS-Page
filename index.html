Who I Am

I’ve been designing digital experiences for over 6 years across a variety of industries. I like to describe myself as more than just a designer. I see myself as a creator of a universe of user stories and images. With a head filled of innovative ideas, I thrive to create eye-catching & user-friendly design solutions provoking emotion in the public. All this on the border between art, craft and technological innovation, always with a piano and a microphone within my arm’s reach to create passionate soothing music.

I am specialized in digital development with a touch of glam rock and a touch of glitter. I manage and oversee the technical aspects of all the projects I work on.

I am passionate about all types of music, cinema. I am also a passionate photographer, singer, and painter.
